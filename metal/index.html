ABOUT METAL ICON

Metal Icon is a metal factory established in 2014 for shop fittings, display systems and metal furniture to meet our customer’s and market needs. Includes production of metal and stainless steel sheets for serving our projects.

PROJECTS

Metal Icon’s project vary through different fields that include pharmacy solutions, metal furniture and store solutions.

PHARMACY SOLUTIONS

Pharmacy solutions are intended to modernise pharmacies by providing customers with a welcoming and comfortable setting that allows customers to seek assistance and advice.  Expert solutions are created to enhance the retail offering. 3D pharmacy visualizations are intended to aid in fully comprehending concepts and spaces.

METAL FURNITURE

We work with designers and retailers to customise our furniture to their clients' and customers' needs, from metal and fabric selection to specifying manufacturing materials, all the way down to customised dimensions. This gallery represents only a small portion of the metal furniture, full collections are available on metal Icon’s social media platforms and showroom.

STORE SOLUTIONS

Metal solutions for storage include various specifics like, guide rails and running rails, face frame profiles, grip rail profiles, profiles for fittings, niche system profiles, connection profiles for worktops, cover profiles, body profiles and many more. The line of products include lockers, racks, pallets, storage units, metal cabinets and many more customizable items.
