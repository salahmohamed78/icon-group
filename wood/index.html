ABOUT WOOD ICON

Wood Icon is a factory specialized in wooden works for commercial and residential scopes including all types of wood and paintings required for our projects. It not only possesses advanced machinery and equipment, but also has a number of high qualified technical support and production management, so as to ensure the quality and style of the finished products are changing with the changing world.

PROJECTS

Projects cover different  architectural woodwork, quality cabinets and fine custom furniture.  Our work includes fine carpentry, cabinet construction, table making, custom millwork, architectural millwork, furniture repair and remodeling.

HOTEL FURNISHING

We manufacture elements where design and durability meet the most appropriate materials, ensuring toughness and ease of maintenance ;furthermore, we are able to offer mass production of components starting from a sample, allowing the completion of entire hotel projects, from seats, tables, and bedroom furnishings, conference rooms, bars and restaurants.

OFFICE FURNITURE

The programme includes a variety of operational and meeting room desks, allowing the model to be incorporated into all spaces and needs, including solutions for senior management. Our teams handle every aspect of workplace management, from design to installation and relocation. Product offered vary between workstations, office sofas, office chairs, meeting tables, cabinets, drawer units and many more.

HOME FURNISHING

The Home Furnishing line adds grace to every corner of your home and offers the most refined selection of fabrics, textures, colours, and prints. Every piece of furniture is designed in-house and manufactured in Egypt by our manufacturing factories. Products offered vary between home sofas, dining tables, dining chairs, bedrooms, doors, drawers, cabinets, TV units, shoe racks, and many more.

RETAIL

Retail renovations offers a diverse selection of stylish and exclusive accessories, such as wood works & claddings, wall art, designer lighting, and much more. Finishes are designed according to current market trends, allowing them to stand out from the crowd of existing ones.
