ABOUT US

Icon group is an Egyptian company based in Cairo since 2005, specialized in importing, manufacturing, distributing and constructing retails, hotels and most of commercial projects.

HISTORY

In 2010 we started our design department for design and smart solutions for different concepts of varieties of shop fittings and decorations.

In 2014 we established our factory for metal and stainless steel (Metal Icon) shop fittings, display systems and metal furniture to meet our customer’s and market needs.

In 2015 our factory for wooden display systems and office furniture (Wood Icon) was established, so we can integrate both wood and metal display systems, to stand with the new trend’s era and meet our valuable customers demand.

In 2017 due to strong need for civil constructing, our company merged with Egyturk merged with ArkiTIK for civil construction, and we established our new brand Display Icon Co. for main contracting.

In 2018 we established MEP (Mechanical Electrical Plumbing) company for specialized contracting. In addition to, residential and interior designs scope (Decor Icon).

In 2021, we established our furniture factory in partnership with one of the top Chinese companies for furniture factories, GWF to complete our profile of turn key solutions and differentiate our group in the market by offering full services in high end commercial projects.

VISION

To provide proven and versatile shop fitting solutions, merchandising systems and shop supplies to the Egyptian market. Our clients value detailed industry knowledge and personal service and our willingness to go above and beyond what is expected. We deliver customized store designs and high-quality products on-time and on-budget.

MISSION

To expand in the Egyptian market, build trust and strong business relationship with major key accounts of retailers. Also, to go beyond expectations.
